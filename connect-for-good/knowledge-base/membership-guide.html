How to Join or Renew

  • Online: Visit our website and select your membership tier.
  • Phone: Call our Member Services line and an agent will process your membership.
  • Chat: Use the chat widget on our website to speak with a representative.
  • Mail: Send a check to our main office with your name, email, and desired tier.

Memberships are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. Renewal reminders are sent 30 days before expiration.

Member Benefits

  • Monthly newsletter with program updates and impact stories
  • Invitations to exclusive member events and galas
  • Discounts at our gift shop and partner organizations
  • Early access to volunteer opportunities and programs
  • Annual impact report showing how your support makes a difference

Cancellation

Memberships can be cancelled at any time by contacting Member Services. Refunds are prorated based on the remaining months in your membership period. Cancellation requests must be made by phone or email.

Updating Your Information

To update your name, address, email, or payment method, contact Member Services or log in to your account on our website.
