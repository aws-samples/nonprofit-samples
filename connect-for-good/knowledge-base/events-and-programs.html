Events and Programs

Upcoming Events

  • Annual Gala: Our flagship fundraising event featuring dinner, live entertainment, and a silent auction. Held every October. Tickets available to members and the public.
  • Community Day: A free family-friendly event with activities, food, and information about our programs. Held quarterly at rotating locations.
  • Volunteer Appreciation Night: An annual celebration honoring our dedicated volunteers. By invitation for active volunteers.
  • Holiday Food Drive: Our largest food collection campaign running November through December. Drop-off locations at all office locations and partner sites.

Ongoing Programs

Food Distribution

We operate 12 community food pantries across the region, serving over 5,000 families monthly. Pantries are open Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday from 9 AM to 1 PM. No appointment or documentation required.

Youth Mentoring

Our mentoring program pairs adult volunteers with young people ages 12-18 for weekly one-on-one sessions focused on academic support, career exploration, and life skills. The program runs year-round with a 6-month minimum commitment.

Disaster Relief

We maintain a rapid response team ready to deploy within 24 hours of a disaster declaration. Services include emergency shelter setup, supply distribution, case management, and long-term recovery support.

Financial Literacy

Free workshops on budgeting, saving, credit building, and homeownership. Offered monthly at our main office and select community locations. Open to the public.
