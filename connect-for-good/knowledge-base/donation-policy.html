Donation Policy

Accepted Donation Types

We accept the following types of donations:

  • One-time donations: Any amount via credit card, debit card, or bank transfer.
  • Recurring donations: Monthly or annual recurring gifts. Cancel anytime by contacting donor services.
  • Corporate matching: We participate in most employer matching programs. Contact your HR department to verify eligibility.
  • In-kind donations: Non-monetary gifts such as supplies, equipment, or professional services. Contact us for approval before shipping.
  • Planned giving: Bequests, charitable trusts, and other estate planning gifts. Contact our planned giving team for assistance.

Tax Deductibility

All monetary donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. We are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Our EIN is 12-3456789.

Donation receipts are emailed automatically within 24 hours of your gift. Annual giving statements are mailed in January for the prior tax year.

For donations of $250 or more, a written acknowledgment is provided as required by the IRS.

Refund Policy

Donations are generally non-refundable. If a donation was made in error, please contact donor services within 30 days for a review. Refund requests are handled on a case-by-case basis.

Donor Privacy

We respect your privacy. Donor information is never sold or shared with third parties. You may choose to remain anonymous for any gift. For our full privacy policy, visit our website.
